Here we provide 10\% of the data from the constructed datasets for blind-reviews.



There are three datasets: Edu-DS, Edu-SM and Edu-PM. These datasets are collected from education field. Each dataset contains concept.txt and pair.xlsx. The label of concept pair <A,B> is 1 means that A is hypernym to B. The label of concept pair <A,B> is 1 means that A is hyponym to B.
